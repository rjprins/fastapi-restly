# Restly has a blog

This is the engineering blog for
[FastAPI-Restly](https://www.fastapi-restly.org/). Posts here cover announcements as well as the
thinking behind the framework. For example why customization handles look the way they do,
how we test, and what we learn from running coding agents against the API.
Follow along via the
[newsletter](https://www.fastapi-restly.org/newsletter.html) or the
[Atom feed](https://www.fastapi-restly.org/blog/posts/atom.xml).
